Privacy Policy

 

Effective Date: October 14, 2017

 

This application collects some personal information from its users. Punwars attempts to collect the most minimal amount of personal information and strives to store even less.

 

Information Punwars accesses:

 

·             The user’s public Facebook profile, including but not limited to:

o   The user’s first name as it appears on Facebook.

o   The user’s public profile picture.

o   The user’s Facebook ID number.

 

Information Punwars stores:

 

·             The user’s first name.

·             The user’s Facebook ID number.

 

Why Punwars collects the information:

·             The user’s first name

o   Personalization and profile identification within the application.

·             The user’s public profile picture

o   Personalization of the user’s profile.

·             The user’s Facebook ID number

o   Acts as a unique identifier for the application to identify the profile and associate actions and progress within the application.

o   The user’s Facebook ID number remains private to any user on the application.

 

Opting-out:

 

·             The user’s first name may be substituted with a user-provided name within the application. This can be done in profile settings.

·             The user’s profile picture may be substituted with an application provided generic profile picture. This can be done in profile settings.

·             The user’s is unable to opt-out of providing their Facebook ID number; however, the user may remove this information from the application by deleting the profile. This can be done in profile settings.

 

Users Under 13:

 

Punwars does not knowingly collect or store any personal information about children under the age of 13. If you believe a child is using this service without parental consent, please contact us at punwars@gmail.com.

 

Policy Updates:

 

This policy may change as Punwars evolves. When the policy changes a notification on the application will be provided to notify you of the change.

 

Contact:

 

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at punwars@gmail.com.
